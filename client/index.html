HEXDAME

DIFFICULTY

ONLINE

RULES

OBJECTIVE

Capture all of your opponent's pieces, or leave them with no legal moves.

MOVEMENT

  • Player 1 (light) moves up-left along the three upper directions.
  • Player 2 (dark) moves down-right along the three lower directions.
  • Pieces move one hex at a time to an empty adjacent hex.

JUMPING

  • Jump over an adjacent enemy piece to an empty hex on the other side to capture it.
  • If a jump is available, you must take it.
  • After a jump, if further jumps are possible from the same piece, you must continue jumping (multi-jump chain).

KINGS

  • A piece reaching the far edge of the board (opponent's back row) is promoted to king.
  • Kings can move and jump in all six hex directions.

WINNING

  • You win by capturing all enemy pieces, or leaving the opponent with no legal move on their turn.

GAME OVER

PAUSED
